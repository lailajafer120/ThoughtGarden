About Me

Hi, I’m **Lila Evergreen**, a mental wellness enthusiast and the creator of ThoughtGarden. I created this space to help you slow down, breathe, reflect, and reconnect with yourself. With a background in psychology and years of experience exploring mindful living, I noticed how deeply people needed a gentle digital corner — a place without pressure, where they can understand their emotions and grow peacefully. ThoughtGarden is that safe corner. Here, you will find tools like mood tracking, journaling prompts, soothing videos, short articles, and simple reflections — all designed to help you build inner clarity, emotional balance, and self-awareness. Let's grow together — one calm breath, one mindful thought, at a time.
